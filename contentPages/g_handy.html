Handy

Clever / November 27, 2020

Handy is a game where the player uses the character’s hands to close holes in the wall and stop the water leak. The fastest player wins this duel to keep his side dry.

Handy is a local competitive game that uses the gamepad’s analog sticks to control each of the character’s hands. Besides closing the wall holes, the player can also grab and throw stones at his opponent to slow him down.

I developed this game during the global game jam 2020, with the colaboration of:

Lucas Maniesi (Sound Designer), Ricardo Minoru Seko Junior (2D Artist), and Wesley Raymundo (Programmer).

Little Shop of Rituals

Clever / November 26, 2020

In this game the player controls Cally, our voodoo expert, to deliver custom-made ritualistic potions to her customers.

When a level starts the customers will deliver an order, and it is your job to follow the instructions to combine the ingredients correctly. Deliver the potion to that customer to get some money and move towards the level goal. You can upgrade your shack to speed up the potion creation process.

I developed this game during the global game jam 2016, with the colaboration of:

Rafael L Lagos (Game Designer) and Ricardo Minoru Seko Junior (2D Artist).

Battle draughts is a checkers-based game with different types of pieces, each one with a unique special ability.

The pieces, and their abilities, are:

• Knight: his ability is a sword slash that hits any selected enemy unit within one square range.
• Archer: shoots an arrow at any selected enemy unit at two non-diagonal squares from the archer.
• Mage: can create a copy of an allied piece in a selected position.

Those abilities get active when a piece performs a capture move.

The game has a single-player mode, with three levels of difficulty, or a local multiplayer.

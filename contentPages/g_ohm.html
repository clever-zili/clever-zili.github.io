Ohm

Clever / November 26, 2020

Protect your astral self to make your connection to the universe. Meditation cannot be interrupted, and the anguishes of the past will do anything to disturb the master’s concentration.

1 / 6

The game has a platformer movement. The main character needs to protect the master using his sword to slash the enemies. When the lights are on, the enemies move faster, so, the player needs to find the balance between knowing where the enemies are and walk in the shadows.

I developed this game during the global game jam 2017, with the colaboration of:

Bruno Sampaio (Programmer), Fernando Scaff Moura (Game designer), Adalberto Rodrigues Junior (Sound designer), Ricardo Minoru Seko Junior (2D Artist), and Saulo Chuchene Bonatto (3D Artist, but for this project 2D Artist).

Control a chicken to help it reach the finish line. Avoid the obstacles and diseases spread along the way, and don't forget to collect the corn and medications to increase your final score.

This runner game, developed in my previous job, is an advergame in which the character's main objective is to reach the finish line. The main character can move between three lines to avoid the obstacles and collect the items to achieve the highest score. The game was used in a fair and did run on a custom arcade machine.

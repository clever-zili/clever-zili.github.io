Infiltrate inside a secret base to retrieve the sacred relic of power. To achieve this goal, you will have to find power-ups and fight against a horde of enemies in this fast-paced dungeon crawler.

Skullki, the main character, can keep only one power-up active at a time, so the player has to choose which one to have, according to their playstyle. Three power-ups are included in this version:

• Slash: a powerful strike with a slow cooldown and short-range.
• Dash: fast strike with lower damage, more focused on mobility than attack, also can negate enemies hazards' damage.
• Energy blast: a ranged attack with a high damage value and a short cooldown, but it reduces the character's life to one point a high-risk, high-reward weapon.
